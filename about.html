About Us

Wares Wardak Hospital, located in the 5th District of Kabul, is dedicated to delivering exceptional health services with compassion and integrity. Since our founding, we've been committed to providing high-quality care for families, individuals, and communities across Afghanistan.

We offer a wide range of services including emergency medicine, diagnostic imaging, pediatrics, surgery, and internal medicine, supported by a team of skilled professionals and modern facilities.

At Wares Wardak Hospital, our mission is simple: to heal with excellence, to comfort with care, and to lead with innovation.
